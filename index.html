Last Runway

Air traffic control under fire

About the game

You're the last air traffic controller for an airport under siege. Drag a path from each incoming plane to a runway and it follows your line — then reroute on the fly to thread past missiles, drones, and anti-missile fire. Land planes to score and survive as long as you can. One crash and the runway is gone.

Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

Drag from any aircraft to a glowing runway to land it. Touch a plane again to redraw its path and dodge incoming threats, or draw a small loop to hold it in place and buy time. Survive as long as you can without losing a plane.

Does the game cost anything?

Last Runway is free to download and play.

Do I need an account or internet connection?

No account is required, and the game can be played offline.

How do I restore my progress?

Your progress is stored on your device. If something looks missing, fully close and reopen the app, and email us if it persists.
